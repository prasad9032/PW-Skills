What is entities

Entities are frequently used to display reserved characters (which would otherwise be interpreted as HTML code), and invisible characters (like non-breaking spaces). You can also use them in place of other characters that are difficult to type with a standard keyboard. Here are some commonly used entities:

  1. less than> - greater than
  2. & - ampersand
  3. " - double quotation mark
  4. ' - single quotation mark
