History
Physics Wallah Private Limited, commonly known as "Physics Wallah" or "PW", was founded in 2016 as a YouTube Channel by Alakh Pandey, an educator hailing from Allahabad, Uttar Pradesh. Along with co-founder Prateek Maheshwari, Pandey developed an app specifically designed for students aspiring to take the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) and Joint Entrance Exam (JEE) in 2020. In addition, Physics Wallah launched courses for School Prep, JEE, NEET, GATE, SSC, UPSC, PSC, NDA, CA Foundation, CA Intermediate, CSIR NET, IIT JAM, MBA, NEET PG, and CUET. As of January 2023, the Physics Wallah app has been downloaded more than 10 million times. Recently,[when?] the company entered the unicorn club with $100 million in funding
